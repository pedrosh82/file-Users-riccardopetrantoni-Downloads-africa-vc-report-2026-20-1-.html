Analyst Take

Africa's funding story in early 2026 is one of more money, less venture. Total capital rose 11% year-on-year to $487M, but equity funding fell 37% while debt soared 165%. The era of VC-led expansion is giving way to a more selective, asset-backed, DFI-driven model — with e-mobility, energy, and logistics replacing fintech as the continent's most bankable bets.
